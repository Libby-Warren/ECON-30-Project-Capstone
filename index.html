Sources and methods

Research notes

Institutional ownership thresholds vary by source. This chart standardizes to share of U.S. single-family rental stock, then interpolates between observed anchors for annual continuity.

  • GAO-24-106643: evidence on late-2011 market structure and 2015 estimated 1-2% SFR share (from 170,000-300,000 institutional homes).
  • Urban Institute estimate (via Callan 2024): 574,000 of 15.1 million U.S. SFR homes in 2022 (about 3.8%).
  • Callan institutional overview used for threshold caveats and ownership definitions.
  • NCREIF/Houlihan Lokey context used only to guide post-2022 trajectory assumptions.
